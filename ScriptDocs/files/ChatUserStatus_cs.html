Class ChatUserStatusPhoton.Chat

Contains commonly used status values for SetOnlineStatus. You can define your own.

フィールド (Variables)

アクセス名前型説明属性・制約
publicSOfflineInt32(0) Offline.
publicSInvisibleInt32(1) Be invisible to everyone. Sends no message.
publicSOnlineInt32(2) Online and available.
publicSAwayInt32(3) Online but not available.
publicSDNDInt32(4) Do not disturb.
publicSLFGInt32(5) Looking For Game/Group. Could be used when you want to be invited or do matchmaking.
publicSPlayingInt32(6) Could be used when in a room, playing.
